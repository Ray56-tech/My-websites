Our Story

Born from
the Soul of Arabia

Zafaran Café was founded with a single vision: to honour the rich coffee traditions of the Arab world while embracing the cosmopolitan spirit of Dubai. Every cup we serve is a dialogue between ancient ritual and modern craft.

Our master roaster sources single-origin beans from Yemen, Ethiopia, and Colombia, blending them with hand-picked saffron, cardamom, and rose from the souks of Deira. The result is a flavour that is unmistakably, beautifully ours.
